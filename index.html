About Us

Who are we?

Thaele's Frozen Custard the best tasting frozen Custard available on the African continent. Thaele's Frozen Custard is the tart frozen Custard company that was created to bring a healthier alternative to ice cream, that also happens to taste better too! Through countless hours of R&D our food scientists concocted the special mix that is quickly becoming a well-known phenomenon in the South African market.
